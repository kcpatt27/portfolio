About

After 8 years producing hip-hop, R&B, and lofi beats, I clicked on a YouTube video about becoming a software developer—and 18 months later, I'm building AI-powered applications. The moment that changed everything? Using ChatGPT to brainstorm flavor combinations while baking cakes, then thinking: "Why not combine this with a place to find recipes?" That lightbulb became NextDish. Years of music production sharpened my intuition, attention to detail, and creative problem-solving—skills that now drive how I architect systems and communicate technical ideas as a DevOps advocate and technical writer.
